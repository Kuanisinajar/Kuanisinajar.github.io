One day at night, I turned off all the lights, pulled the curtain in my room, went into bed and coverd myself with a sheet and pillows. It supposed to be calm and quite yet a sound in my head slightly cut off the silence.

“My heart is empty,” it said. No echo.

It is, indeed. At that period, I could not feel happiness nor sadness. I didn’t have anything that must do. Most of my friends were thousand miles away. And I didn’t like myself that much.
“I have to fill something inside. And I have to prevent those from draining away.” I thought.

//

I started to code. The first line is “myHeartIsEmpty = true”.
I decided to write a line every day. It looks like this: [6, true, false, false, false]
And it means: [How I feel about today(1-10), did I make friends happy, did I have any achievements, did I see improvements of myself, did I like myself a bit more]
These codes will be calculated and printed as a dot in the blank page. Through days and the accumulating data it will change the opacity. Generally, if I keep having false, the dots will fade away. Otherwise it will become more solid.
The goal is to turn the first line into “myHeartIsEmpty = false”
This is also a blog-like website. Click on every dot and the log will show.
All in all, hopefully through this project, the blank page will fill with things, as so my heart will be.

//

I would use some commands to check with the performance.
If you like, please type "command()" in your browser console to interact with it.
To open the console, simply right click anywhere and hit the “Inspect/Inspect Elements”. In the interface, click on “console” and you can type in the commands in the area.

某天夜晚，我關上房間的電燈，拉上窗簾，上床，讓棉被、枕頭還有床墊將我安全地包起來。那應該能夠帶給我平靜，然而我腦中的聲音卻劃破了沈寂。

「My heart is empty(我的心是空的。)」它這樣說，沒有殘響。

確實。在那段期間，沒有事情使我開心抑或難過、沒有任何非得達成的目標，大多數的朋友都在半個地球那麼遠的距離之外，而我也並不是那麼喜歡自己。
我想往裡面填些東西。而我還得防止他們如往常那樣快速流失。

//

我開始寫程式。第一行即是：myHeartIsEmpty = true;
每天我會寫下一行程式碼，記錄當天的狀態。它看起來像是這樣：[6, true, false, false, false]
意思則是：[當天的幸福指數(1-10), 是否讓朋友開心, 是否有成就, 是否看見自己改善了什麼, 是否多喜歡自己一點]
這些數據會被計算後成為網頁裡的一個小點。並且隨著每天增加的數據而去改變它的透明度。簡單來說的話，如果我一直得到false，它就會漸漸消失；反之，則會越來越濃。
最終的目標是希望經過運算後，第一行變成：myHeartIsEmpty = false;
這同時也是個部落格的變型體。點擊每個圓點的話可以看見當天的日誌。
總之，希望這個空白的頁面可以漸漸填滿，我的心也是。

//
