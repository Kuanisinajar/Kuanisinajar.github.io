One day at night, I turned off all the lights, pulled the curtain in my room, went into bed and coverd myself with a sheet and pillows. It supposed to be calm and quite yet a sound in my head slightly cut off the silence.

“My heart is empty,” it said. No echo.

It is, indeed. At that period, I could not feel happiness nor sadness. I didn’t have anything that must do. Most of my friends were thousand miles away. And I didn’t like myself that much.
“I have to fill something inside. And I have to prevent those from draining away.” I thought.

//

I started to code. The first line is “myHeartIsEmpty = true”. To make it turn false, I set up some basic rules: every day I will check if I fulfill making friends happy, if I had achieve something, if I saw improvements of myself and if I love myself a little bit more. The performance will be caculated and printed in the webpage as a dot. If I keep failing, the dots will disapear. If I make enough success, my heart will no longer be empty.

//

If you click on the dots, it will show the daily log.
If you like, please type "command()" in your browser console to interact with it.

某天夜晚，我關上房間的電燈，拉上窗簾，上床，讓棉被、枕頭還有床墊將我安全地包起來。那應該能夠帶給我平靜，然而我腦中的聲音卻劃破了沈寂。

「My heart is empty(我的心是空的。)」它這樣說，沒有殘響。

確實。在那段期間，沒有事情使我開心抑或難過、沒有任何非得達成的目標，大多數的朋友都在半個地球那麼遠的距離之外，而我也並不是那麼喜歡自己。
我想往裡面填些東西。而我還得防止他們如往常那樣快速流失。

//

我開始寫程式。第一行即是：myHeartIsEmpty = true;
我寫下些規則：每天我會檢視自己是否做到讓朋友感到開心、是否有些成就、是否看見自己改善了什麼以及是否多喜歡自己一點。這些會被記錄在每天的一行程式碼裡，並且運算後成為網頁裡的一個圓點。如果我沒有達成，這些圓點將會慢慢消失；反之，它們會越來越明顯，我的心也就不會是空的了吧。
